Introduction to BTS

BTS stands for Bangtan Boys, Bulletproof Boy Scouts Bangtan Sonyeondan and Beyond the scene.Hitman Bang PD is the founder and CEO of BigHit entertainment. BTS is a Korean boyband, who worked hard to reach the top and become who they are today.There are seven members in the band, which are jin, RM, yoongi,hobi, jimin, taehyung and jungkook.They debuted 13th June 2013 with the song No More Dream.

At the start, they were not really known because they came from a small company, which was at the edge of bankruptcy, but they started to become a success in the in the era of I Need You and Dope in 2015, but they still had loyal fans from the beginning. This era was when they got their first win in a music show. After this era, they started to rise and from then on, they have just been going higher with each comeback.

BTS write their own songs and they talk about controversial issues, that affect young people such as mental health, they also criticize they society in which we live in. The genre's that BTS do is k-pop hip-hop and R&B EDM. RM has written he most songs for the group. They all take part in writing songs, especially their solo songs. There songs are connected, which is why the theories is really strong in the fandom.
